Strategic Energy Shield (SES)

Powering the AI Age from Space

SES is a revolutionary space-based solar power array designed to beam clean, abundant energy directly to Earth and beyond. Built for the age of artificial intelligence, climate urgency, and national resilience.

The Mission

To ensure the United States and its allies achieve permanent energy independence by deploying the world’s first orbital solar array—capable of powering entire nations with zero emissions.

How It Works

  • Massive photovoltaic array in geostationary orbit
  • Power beamed to Earth via microwave or laser transmission
  • Autonomous and human-assisted construction in orbit
  • Redundant satellite-to-Earth relays and battery storage

Why Now?

With global AI workloads exploding and terrestrial renewables falling short, SES provides a scalable, ultra-reliable solution—unaffected by night, weather, or geopolitics.

Powering the AI Age from Space

The Strategic Energy Shield is a next-generation orbital solar energy system engineered to deliver uninterrupted, secure power to Earth and Mars. It’s our moonshot for a renewable, AI-driven future.

The Mission

SES will secure American energy independence through a scalable, modular solar array stationed in geostationary orbit. Designed for resilience, built to power the next century.

How It Works

  • Orbiting 4,000+ km² solar array with autonomous and human-assisted expansion
  • Energy transmission via phased microwave or laser relay satellites
  • Ground receivers + megabattery infrastructure for uninterrupted baseload power
  • Robotic and human fabrication hubs in orbit

Why Now?

AI workloads are exploding. Grid reliability is declining. Terrestrial renewables are intermittent and geopolitically fragile. SES offers a sovereign, scalable energy solution ready to meet future global demands.

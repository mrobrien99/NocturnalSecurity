About Nocturnal Security

Nocturnal Security, LLC is a mission-driven technology company focused on advancing planetary defense, energy infrastructure, and public safety through AI-powered systems. Founded by Spencer O’Brien, our goal is to develop ambitious, resilient technologies that address humanity’s most urgent challenges—both on Earth and beyond.

Our core initiatives include:

  • Vigilant Front – A next-generation AI-assisted lockdown system for schools and high-risk facilities.
  • Strategic Energy Shield (SES) – A space-based solar energy array that collects and beams power to Earth and Mars.

We believe in delivering bold, futuristic solutions rooted in strong engineering, tactical simplicity, and real-world survivability.
